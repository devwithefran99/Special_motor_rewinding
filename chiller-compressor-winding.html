What We Do

Winding That Gets Your Chiller Cold Again

A burnt-out chiller compressor can shut down your entire cooling system — and a brand-new unit is expensive and slow to source. We rewind the motors inside hermetic and semi-hermetic chiller compressors, matching the original winding specification exactly so your system runs at full cooling capacity again.

Each compressor is checked for winding shorts and insulation breakdown, rewound with high-temperature copper and refrigerant-safe insulation, then vacuum-sealed and load-tested in our Mussafah workshop to make sure it holds up under continuous cooling duty.

How We Work

Our Winding Process

How we take a failed chiller compressor and return it sealed, tested, and ready to cool — step by step.

  1. 01

    Fault Diagnosis

    We open and test the compressor to confirm the burnt or shorted winding, then give you a clear quote.

  2. 02

    Rewinding To Spec

    The motor is rewound with high-temperature copper and refrigerant-safe insulation, matched to the original spec.

  3. 03

    Vacuum & Seal

    The compressor is vacuum-dried, sealed, and leak-checked so no moisture or refrigerant escapes in use.

  4. 04

    Load Test & Handover

    We load-test under cooling duty, confirm it holds capacity, and return it with a workmanship warranty.
