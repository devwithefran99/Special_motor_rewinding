What We Do

Rewinding For Every Motor Type

Whether it's a single-phase AC motor, a three-phase industrial unit, or a DC motor, a burnt winding doesn't mean the end of the motor. We rewind AC and DC motors of every voltage and power rating, matching the original specification so the motor runs at full performance again.

Every motor is checked for shorts, earth faults, and insulation breakdown, then rewound by hand with high-grade copper and quality insulation, and load-tested and balanced in our Mussafah workshop before it goes back to you.

How We Work

Our Rewinding Process

How we take a burnt-out AC or DC motor and return it rewound, balanced, and running like new — step by step.

  1. 01

    Winding Inspection

    We test the coils and insulation to find the burnt or shorted windings, then give you a clear quote.

  2. 02

    Strip & Rewind

    Old windings are removed, the core is cleaned, and fresh copper coils are wound to the original spec.

  3. 03

    Varnish & Cure

    The new windings are insulated and varnish-dipped for heat and moisture resistance, then oven-cured.

  4. 04

    Test & Handover

    The rewound motor is load-tested and balanced, then returned with a workmanship warranty.
