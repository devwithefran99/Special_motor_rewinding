Our Story

A Repair Shop Built Around One Honest Question

Motor Service started with a simple idea: when a motor fails, the shop owner deserves a straight answer — repair it, rewind it, or replace it — not an upsell. That honesty is why workshops, water suppliers, and small factories across Mussafah keep coming back.

Today our engineers rewind and repair AC & DC motors, chiller compressors, water pumps, generators, and power tools. Every job leaves the bench tested, balanced, and covered by a workmanship warranty.

  • Honest advice — we'll tell you if a repair isn't worth it
  • Fast turnaround, most repairs in 1–3 working days
  • Workmanship warranty on every rewinding job
