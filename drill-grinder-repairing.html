What We Do

Repairs That Bring Your Tools Back To Power

A drill or grinder that won't run or has lost power usually has a burnt armature, worn carbon brushes, or a failed switch — not a reason to throw the whole tool away. We repair and rewind drill machines, angle grinders, and power tools of all brands, bringing them back to full working condition.

Each tool is opened up and checked for armature faults, worn brushes, bearings, gears, and switch problems, then rewound and rebuilt with quality parts and tested in our Mussafah workshop before it goes back to you.

How We Work

Our Repair Process

How we take a dead drill or grinder and return it rewound, rebuilt, and running at full power — step by step.

  1. 01

    Open & Diagnose

    We open the tool and check the armature, brushes, bearings, and switch to find the fault, then quote you.

  2. 02

    Rewind Armature

    A burnt armature is rewound with quality copper to the original spec to restore full torque and speed.

  3. 03

    Replace & Rebuild

    Worn brushes, bearings, gears, and switches are replaced and the tool is reassembled with care.

  4. 04

    Test & Handover

    The tool is run-tested for full power and safe operation, then returned with a workmanship warranty.
