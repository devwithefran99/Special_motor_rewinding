What We Do

Rewinding That Brings Your Motor Back To Life

When a motor burns out, replacing it isn't your only option. Our team rewinds AC and DC electric motors of every size — from small single-phase pumps to large three-phase industrial units — using high-grade copper windings and quality insulation materials that restore full efficiency.

Every rewind is done by hand by experienced engineers, then load-tested and balanced before it leaves our Mussafah workshop. The result is a motor that runs like new, at a fraction of the cost and wait time of buying a replacement.

How We Work

Our Rewinding Process

How we take a burnt-out electric motor and bring it back to full running condition — step by step.

  1. 01

    Winding Inspection

    We test the coils and insulation to find the burnt or shorted windings, then give you a clear rewind quote.

  2. 02

    Stripping & Cleaning

    The old windings are removed and the stator core is cleaned and baked, ready to take fresh copper coils.

  3. 03

    Rewinding & Varnish

    New copper coils are wound to the original spec, insulated, and varnish-dipped for heat and moisture resistance.

  4. 04

    Testing & Handover

    The rewound motor is load-tested, balanced, and returned with a workmanship warranty — pickup & delivery included.
