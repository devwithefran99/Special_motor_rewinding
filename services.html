How We Work

Our Repair Process

A clear, step-by-step workflow — from the moment your motor arrives to the day it runs like new.

  1. 01

    Inspection & Quote

    We inspect your motor, identify the fault, and give you a clear no-obligation cost estimate before any work starts.

  2. 02

    Rewinding & Repair

    Our technicians rewind coils, replace worn bearings, and repair faults using quality insulated materials.

  3. 03

    Testing & Balancing

    Every motor is load-tested and balanced to make sure it runs smoothly, safely, and at full efficiency.

  4. 04

    Delivery & Warranty

    We hand back your motor with a workmanship warranty — pickup and delivery available across Abu Dhabi.
