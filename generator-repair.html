What We Do

Repairs That Keep Your Power On

When a generator fails, you lose your backup power exactly when you need it most. We repair diesel and petrol generators — rewinding the alternator, fixing the AVR and control faults, and replacing worn parts so your set delivers stable, full-rated power again.

Each generator is fault-diagnosed end to end — alternator windings, AVR, wiring, and connections — then rewound and rebuilt with quality parts and load-tested in our Mussafah workshop to confirm it holds voltage and frequency under load.

How We Work

Our Repair Process

How we take a dead generator and return it rewound, tested, and ready to deliver stable power — step by step.

  1. 01

    Full Diagnosis

    We test the alternator, AVR, wiring, and engine connections to find every fault, then give you a clear quote.

  2. 02

    Rewind & Repair

    The alternator is rewound to spec and the AVR, control, and worn parts are repaired or replaced with quality parts.

  3. 03

    Reassemble & Wire

    The generator is reassembled and rewired correctly so every connection is safe and secure before testing.

  4. 04

    Load Test & Handover

    We load-test to confirm steady voltage and frequency, then return the generator with a workmanship warranty.
